UI/UX DESIGNER

ROLE IN RHAMJET

As a UI/UX Designer in RHAMJET, you will be expected to design and shape the experience of our application RhamScan for users. This will involve collaborating with a whole team to evaluate the current designs of the application and measure its usability, designing new UIs and refining old UIs to better fit the needs of the user and testing and working with the programming developers to ensure a smooth implementation of the updates.

Additionally, you will be working with museums and art curator clients to design specialised galleries for real life galleries and ensuring the application works in conjunction with their designs while maintaining the RHAMScan application to encompass those designs.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Research, analysis, wireframes, journey mapping, prototyping and testing
- Lead and conduct relevant user research activities (qualitative and quantitative)
- Pair with engineers to transform components and patterns into working artifacts for implementation, experience with design
- Collaborate with product, QA, and engineering teams to establish UI pattern usage and industry best practices
- Provide UX design for new/enhanced stories

SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE REQUIRED

- Solid understanding of the width and depth of user centred design thinking methodology
- Experiences in conducting user research agile frameworks and design sprints
- Awareness of emerging UI patterns and staying ahead of the curve
- Competence in Figma or similar design tools
- Experience with design systems, accessibility, and localisation
- Coding is not required but it will be a huge bonus
- Curiosity in neomorphism and motion design
