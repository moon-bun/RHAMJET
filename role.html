ROLES

Upon receiving the assignment 3 brief and evaluating our assignment specifications, it was decided that team RHAMJET would be split into two groups: a Research team and a Technologies (Tech) team. Research would be responsible for the bulk of report writing, everything regarding RhamScan as a project and RHAMJET's processes as a team. The Tech team would be responsible for our artifacts, and the sections of our report related to the artifacts. All assignment 3 tasks were evaluated according to these criteria and designated to each team.

RHAMJET decided on two leadership positions. Research Lead, and a Lead Developer with an Assistant Developer. Our Research Lead, Elliot, manages the Research Team. He oversees all Research tasks to maintain coherence between sections. RhamScan as a project was developed from the seed of Elliot's assignment 1 project idea, thus Elliot is the most qualified team member to proofread work written about the project. Research team members include Hunter, Moon, and Roma.

Our Lead Developer, Abel, manages our Tech Team in the same way. Abel is the best option for Lead Developer as the most technically knowledgeable member of RHAMJET. Tech team members include Jamie, Roma, and Tyler.

Jamie is our Assistant Developer and a secondary leader within the Tech Team. A second lead is necessary due to the scope of the Tech Teams responsibilities (mainly due to the multiple tech artefacts). Team positions are loosely defined and flexible. All members are able to outsource help from other members, but our roles provide general direction and shorthand for determining skillsets. As work is completed, team members are assigned further tasks.

Nearing the end of the assignment, a Design Team was put together for the creation of our website. This consisted of Roma, Moon, and Abel. These people were best suited for the position considering availability and skillsets.
