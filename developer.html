FULL STACK DEVELOPER

ROLE IN RHAMJET

As a Full-Stack Developer at RHAMJET, your responsibilities entail overseeing both sides of the web application and facilitating communication between both front-end and back-end development, while also being flexible enough to assist either development ends in finishing tasks. Meanwhile, this position is also more of a leader role, as you will also assist in the planning phase of developing new features to utilise your knowledge of web and mobile application development and will assist in connecting the application to the servers and databases created for it.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Technical oversight of applications
- Developing and maintaining new features for the new app
- Leading technical architecture and coding standards
- Developing quality software
- Writing unit, system, and integration tests for various software components
