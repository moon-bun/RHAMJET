DATABASE ADMINISTRATOR

ROLE IN RHAMJET

As a Database Administrator in RHAMJET, your responsibilities will be to oversee and direct the development and maintenance of the RhamScan databases that are integral to its function. As such, you will spend your time at RHAMJET ensuring the mobile application has an ongoing access to the database that stores the key data such as models, artwork types and so on. As well, it is your role to design and implement policies and protocols on the database itself, ensuring security and maintenance is not compromised in production. As well, as the game expands in popularity and demand for its release in new mobile markets occurs, it is your responsibility to implement and expand the database system to meet those needs.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Install and configuration of SQLite
- Configure and maintain database servers and processes
- Monitor system health and performance, to ensure high levels of performance, availability, and security.
- Perform scheduled maintenance and support
- Making sure the company data stores run without interruption.
- Manage data growth while keeping costs down.
- Keeping the database and related software up to date.

SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE REQUIRED

- Having a strong team spirit, helping other team members as and when required
- Having capability to self-managing in a dynamic environment and adapt to change
- Being able to easily learn and adapt to new technologies
- Being flexible and capable of working out of hours, as required
- 3 to 5 years of experience working on database design, development and administration.
- Having knowledge of database design and development experience on tables, views, stored procedures, functions and security permissions.
- Experience using any SQL Server in their recent versions (Preferably SQLite)
- Bachelor's degree in an IT related field
- Being a team player, proactive and passionate toward the work
